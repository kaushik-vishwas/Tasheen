About Us

TASHEEN A SACRED LUXURY

The exploration of the far-reaches of the continents has revealed beautiful traditional techniques and design that are hard to find in stores, all while using the rarest fabrics the world has to offer and committing to sustainability. Tasheen is putting forward new solutions to existing problems. The fashion industry is a major contributor to global air and water pollution, atrocious wastage, animal and human exploitation – and that’s just the tip of the unethical iceberg. We work on extracting fibers from unusual plants and make clothes with minimum impact on nature. We are taking major steps towards shifting from anything that strains the planet in the name of fashion to promoting harmless, compostable, and sustainable fabrics for your clothes (and nature). With so much secrecy in the world of fashion, we offer utmost transparency as to how each of our pieces came to be.

From the lotus stem to the orange peel, the Tasheen brand is turning waste to value.

‘TASHEEN's intention is to combine old-world skills with contemporary design to create sophisticated pieces that are at once traditional and modern. In working with local artisans from remote and inspiring cultures, TASHEEN gives the world access to the uniqueness of these traditions that might otherwise remain unseen, providing artisans with a livelihood that enables them to keep their traditional skills and culture alive for the benefit of humanity.

TASHEEN believes in the integrity of design. We understand the hard-work and love that goes into creation, and we believe all things should endure in both quality and aesthetic.

Every thread, every fibre, every button is accounted for, because we have nothing to hide. We don’t just talk about ethics and sustainability, we live and breathe them. Our clothes are made in India from decomposable, organic and recycled materials. We bring you the rarest fabrics in the world and work keenly to ensure that every step of the journey is as thoughtful and sustainable as possible. With new fibers, the problem isn’t the science, it’s convincing people that there’s a healthy demand for alternatives—and making sure there’s zero compromise in performance.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy originates with a shift in our thinking—about the way we design, the materials we select, and their impact on the environment. Each collection is created, first and foremost, to bring something new, special, and lasting into the world. Our commitment to sustainability goes down to the fiber itself. We work closely to source and create one-of-a-kind fabrics that are as luxurious as they are conscientious.

Maximizing Our Efforts

Our goal for the future is to continue on the path of being more sustainable and environmentally responsible. Our core purpose will always be the same - being thoughtful of how we design, the materials we select and their impact on the environment. Looking ahead, our commitment is to minimize our impact on the planet, be transparent in our supply chain processes and be more innovative with our sustainability.

Our Materials

We believe nature is the ultimate luxury. At the heart of TASHEEN is a focus on fabrics. All materials are sourced with an eye toward environmental good, whether organic and natural fabrics, reclaimed fibers that conserve material resources or trims made by artisan collectives in the developing world. Each piece in the collection embodies a singular philosophy: that mindful living will always be in style.

Our essence is in an intricate weave of unique artistry and enduring style. By selecting the finest fashion and crafts from the planet’s most remote corners, our dream is to become a touch point to the planet’s best unique artisanal creations using the rarest fabrics the world has to offer.

TRADITION AND HERITAGE

Craftsmanship is the new luxury. We are committed to showcase and celebrate traditional and artisanal expertise, skills, processes and practices. We love respecting all cultural heritage throughout the design and development of each of our unique pieces.

Traveling has always been one of the greatest ways to enrich oneself with both cultural knowledge and precious memories. By unleashing complex emotions, it holds the power to make us fall in love with the culture of a country different from our own. The travel experience extends to the connection with the hi and heritage of a new place directly through its craftsmanship and this is precisely what Tasheen enables.
